Program Features

Highlights

  • In this AICTE approved program, you will get a strong business foundation, and learn analytics techniques
  • Get in-depth exposure to analytical tools – Python, Tableau, Excel
  • Practice what you learnt with the Capstone project: for real-life strategy development
  • Get support and handholding with your personal mentor, academic fellows & strong network of peers
  • Get a deep understanding of the Industry by solving real industry problems via sponsored projects with detailed personalised feedback

Outcomes

  • Effectively learn to derive business insights through analytics with tools like Excel, Python, Tableau
  • Be able to formulate the Analytics question by identifying and understanding the business problem
  • Apply Data visualization tools for effective communication to a diverse audience with analytical solutions across a range of functional areas and industry verticals
  • Analyse the various analytical approaches and select the most appropriate for the problem at hand
