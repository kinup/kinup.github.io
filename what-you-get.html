UGC Entitled | Blended & ODL Mode
All programmes offered are UGC entitled, and are delivered in the open and distance learning mode. Hence you can pursue a programme with us without having to alter /modify your current occupation or engagements. You get a rich learning experience through the latest ed-tech platform used to deliver live online sessions, and the extremely user-friendly student dashboard. As a student of NMIMS Global, you get to learn from the finest academic minds that unlocks your true potential.
