Who we are

Our Genesis
Karma Education was conceptualized in 2008 by first generation entrepreneurs with a philosophy that: ‘once you get the student interested in the subject, the teaching ends and the LEARNING begins’.
Over the years, all of us at Karma have endeavored to set up systems and processes to adhere to this philosophy and have successfully done so. This philosophy also forms the underlying essence in each conversation that you may have with any team-member of Karma. We are based out of Mumbai and thanks to the digitalized world that we live in, we serve students from across India and the world.

And if it may interest you, about our constitution - we are a Company duly registered with the Ministry of Company Affairs (M.C.A.) and are fully complaint with all its rules and regulations.

Our NGA-SCE Association

NMIMS Global Access School for Continuing Education, or NGA-SCE, is a part of the prestigious SVKM’S NMIMS Deemed-to-be-University. Karma Education signed up with NGA-SCE as an ‘Authorized Enrolment Partner’ (AEP), in the year 2014. As an AEP our role is to help and support students in all aspects involved in their learning journey of a program offered by NGA-SCE. It could be an MBA, BBA, M.Sc. or a Diploma /Certificate program – Karma Education as an AEP hand-holds the student right from filling up the admission form and up to receiving the final degree certificate.
