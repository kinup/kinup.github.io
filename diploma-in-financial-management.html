Program Features

Highlights

  • The one-year program covers the functional areas of financial management with quantitative and conceptual inputs.
  • Highlights the financial concepts, processes, and operations from a managerial perspective.
  • Understand concepts such as cost accounting, financial statements, etc.

Outcomes

  • Use analytical thinking, continuous process, managerial decision making to maintain a balance between risk & profitability, coordination process, etc.
  • Demonstrate skills for financial learning, planning, and management at a managerial level.
  • Effectively manage financial needs and business operations.

Get an edge over the competition with the right tools and resources, to build a successful career in the financial markets. The program prepares you to take up various roles such as:

Financial Planner
Evaluate and analyse individuals and businesses and guide them to take appropriate budgetary, savings and investment decisions.
Financial Auditor
Gather information from the organisation’s account balances, cash flow statements, income statements, etc.
Financial Planning Analyst
Manage the company’s cash flow, by assessing both quantitative and qualitative aspects of the company and define its goals and to map out the future strategy.
