Program Features

Highlights

  • The two-year M.B.A degree program (Blended and ODL mode) introduces the concepts used in supply chain management and delves deeper into the theory and application of it.
  • Apply the concepts of operations to the principles of business management
  • Illustrate the value of procurement, lean and warehouse management, logistics and inventory management in creating an efficient supply chain
  • Understand the processes behind managing logistics while leveraging technology to maximise the output
  • Optimise processes to control costs and meet industry standards
  • Understand the importance of large- and small-scale inventory management.
  • Successful completion of this program shall entitle you to a Master of Business Administration (Distance) degree certification

Outcomes

  • Facilitate the development of a company through a more effective SCM process
  • Streamline the functions within broader operation networks and supply chains.
  • Effectively manage operation capacities to identify mismatches between demand and supply.
  • Manage supply chain operations to ensure a “flow” of products and services to deliver what the customers want- at the price they want and the quantity they need.
  • Effectively tackle supply chain problems and manage opportunities and challenges in an internationally operated business.
