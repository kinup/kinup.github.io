Program Features

Highlights

  • The two-semester program teaches you the basic functionality of financial firms, banks, stock markets, etc.
  • Acquaint yourself with various financial institutions and their regulations.
  • Focused on developing skills such as financial analysis, cost accounting, management accounting, and general decision making.

Outcomes

  • Understand day to day operations and activities of banking and finance institutions.
  • Understand financial institutions and their role in the financial markets.
  • Be equipped with knowledge of marketing concepts and activities related to the special requirements of financial services.
