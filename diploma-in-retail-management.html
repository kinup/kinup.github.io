Program Features

Highlights

  • The one-year program familiarises you with the various activities that are involved in the retail sector
  • Apply concepts of operations management such as Warehouse Planning, Operations & Supply Chain Strategies, and New Product Development.
  • A broad overview of different aspects of retail management like merchandising, consumer behaviour, etc.

Outcomes

  • Develop conceptual skills required in the Retail Sector.
  • Effectively manage large scale operations, mass inventory and multiple functional activities.
  • Ability to develop a marketing plan for a new or existing product or service by integrating marketing concepts, strategies, research, and distribution principles for retail.

Retail Manager
Manage statistical and financial records, as well as deal with customer queries and grievances. Plus, supervise pricing and stock holdings by optimising profitability and achieving sales targets.
Merchandising Manager
Coordinate and connect with company buyers to purchase the right items to complete the store’s inventory.
Store Manager
Schedule and assign employees and manage store staff recruitment, selection, orientation, and training.
