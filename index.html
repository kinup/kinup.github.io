Who we are

Karma Education was conceptualized in 2008 by first generation entrepreneurs with a philosophy that: ‘once you get the student interested in the subject, the teaching ends and the LEARNING begins’

Over the years, all of us at Karma have endeavored to set up systems and processes to adhere to this philosophy and have successfully done so. This philosophy also forms the underlying essence in each conversation that you may have with any team-member of Karma.

We are based out of Mumbai and thanks to the digitalized world that we live in, we serve students from across India and the world.

WHAT WE OFFER

UGC Entitled | Blended & ODL Mode All Programs offered are UGC entitled, and are delivered in the open and distance learning mode. Hence you can pursue a programme with us without having to alter /modify your current occupation or engagements. You get a rich learning experience through the latest ed-tech platform used to deliver live online sessions, and the extremely user-friendly student dashboard. As a student of NMIMS Global, you get to learn from the finest academic minds that unlocks your true potential. Specializations Explore the range of specialisations offered by the post-graduation programs and we are sure that you will find one that suits you the best. Get connected to any of our admission advisors in case you need any help in finding what will suit you the best.
