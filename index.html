Who we are

Karma Education was conceptualized in 2008 by first generation entrepreneurs with a philosophy that: ‘once you get the student interested in the subject, the teaching ends and the LEARNING begins’

Over the years, all of us at Karma have endeavored to set up systems and processes to adhere to this philosophy and have successfully done so. This philosophy also forms the underlying essence in each conversation that you may have with any team-member of Karma.

We are based out of Mumbai and thanks to the digitalized world that we live in, we serve students from across India and the world.
