Program Features

Highlights

  • The 6-month program familiarises you with the field of corporate communication, which enables you to think of communication in a strategic and global manner.
  • Trains you to create a cohesive workplace culture and build its competencies.
  • Sharpen Communication Skills, strategic, critical and applied thinking.

Outcomes

  • Understand the strategic role of corporate communications for business operations, issues and crisis management.
  • Effectively Plan, research, write and implement everything from strategic communications plans to newsletters.
  • Understand how communications strategies influence employees and stakeholders.
  • Learn how to connect and liaison with the PR industry.
Subjects
Management Theory and Practice
Mass Communication
Written and Oral Communication
Public Relations Theory and Practice
Business Communication
Integrated Marketing Communications

Get an edge over the competition with the right tools and resources, to forge a successful business management career. The program prepares you to take up various roles such as:

Media Relations Manager
Responsible for preparing articles and press releases to promote the brand/product. Also, cultivate and enhance working relationships with the press.
Public Relations Manager
Field media questions and pitch stories to media as well as organise press conferences to ensure visibility.
